### The Challenge
As one of the largest state transportation agencies in the U.S., VDOT faced the challenge of managing a vast and complex IT landscape. Without a centralized Enterprise Architecture (EA) function, technology investments were often siloed, leading to redundant systems, inconsistent data standards, and a lack of alignment with the agency's long-term strategic goals.

### The Strategy: Modern Governance
I was brought in to stand up a modern EA practice from the ground up. My approach focused on moving away from "ivory tower" architecture toward a collaborative, value-driven governance model. Key initiatives included:

*   **Governance Framework Implementation**: Established the standards, policies, and review processes (Architecture Review Board) required to ensure all IT projects align with agency-wide business priorities.
*   **Cloud-First Strategy**: Championed the secure and efficient adoption of Azure-native solutions, providing a roadmap for migrating legacy on-premises workloads to the cloud.
*   **Product Line Alignment**: Worked directly with business leaders to align IT product line roadmaps with VDOT's mission-critical operations, from maintenance and operations to engineering and construction.

### Architectural Oversight
Beyond policy, I provide direct architectural oversight for the agency's most critical platforms. This involves:

*   **Budget Forecasting**: Aligning technical roadmaps with multi-year budget cycles to ensure sustainable funding for modernization efforts.
*   **Security by Design**: Integrating security standards early in the architectural lifecycle to protect the Commonwealth's transportation data and infrastructure.
*   **Interoperability Standards**: Defining the integration patterns and data standards required to break down silos and enable seamless data flow across the agency.

### The Impact
The establishment of the EA practice has provided VDOT with a clear technical North Star. By aligning IT investments with business strategy, we have improved the agency's ability to deliver modern, secure, and cost-effective transportation solutions for the citizens of Virginia.
