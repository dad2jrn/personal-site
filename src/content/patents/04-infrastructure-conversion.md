### Plain English Summary
Moving large-scale IT systems to the cloud is notoriously difficult and expensive. This patent describes a system designed to simplify and optimize that migration process.

The invention works by analyzing an organization's existing, "old-school" IT setup and automatically generating several different "blueprints" for how that same system could look in the cloud. It doesn't just copy the old setup; it looks for ways to make the new cloud version more efficient, cheaper, and more reliable. This allows companies to see their options and choose the best path forward for their specific business needs, making the transition to the cloud much smoother and more cost-effective.

### Key Innovation
The automated generation of multiple optimized "proposal configurations" based on an analysis of existing infrastructure, providing a data-driven roadmap for cloud migration and optimization.
